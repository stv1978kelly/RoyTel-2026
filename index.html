⛳ Stroke Play Selected
Every shot counts. Lowest total score wins.
Rules:
  • Each player counts every stroke from tee to hole
  • Handicap strokes deducted from gross total at the end
  • Lowest net score wins
  • Ties broken by back 9, then back 6, then back 3
🏆 Stableford Selected
Points per hole based on score vs par. Most points wins.
Points:
  • Albatross (3 under) = 5 pts
  • Eagle (2 under) = 4 pts
  • Birdie (1 under) = 3 pts
  • Par = 2 pts
  • Bogey (1 over) = 1 pt
  • Double bogey or worse = 0 pts
Handicap strokes applied per Stroke Index before calculating points.
🤝 Texas Scramble Selected
Team format played in groups of 2–4. One team score per hole.
Rules:
  • All players tee off on each hole
  • The best drive is selected by the team
  • All players play their next shot from that spot
  • This continues until the ball is holed
  • Each team member must contribute a minimum number of drives
  • Minimum drives = holes ÷ players (rounded down)
  • Team handicap = 25% of lowest HCP + 20% of 2nd + 15% of 3rd + 10% of highest
